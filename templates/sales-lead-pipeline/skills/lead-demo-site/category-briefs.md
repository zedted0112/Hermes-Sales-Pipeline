# Category briefs — business-specific demo sites

Use these when building `lead-demo-site`. The goal is **their business, their vibe** — not a generic template.

---

## Gym / fitness

**Feel:** Bold, energetic, confident. Dark or high-contrast accents. Movement.

**Hero copy pattern:**
- "{Business Name} — Transform Your Fitness in {City}"
- Sub: "Personal training • Group classes • Results that last"

**Sections:**
1. Hero + "Book free trial" CTA
2. Programs (Strength, Cardio, HIIT, Yoga — pick 3–4)
3. Why us (3 bullets: trainers, equipment, community)
4. Membership teaser (Starter / Pro / Elite — prices optional "Contact us")
5. Location + hours + map placeholder
6. Footer: phone, WhatsApp, address from lead data

**Colors:** Deep charcoal + electric orange/red OR navy + gold. Avoid pastel spa colors.

**If lead lacks website/booking:** Make "Book Free Trial" and WhatsApp button the visual hero of the page.

---

## Beauty salon / spa

**Feel:** Elegant, calm, premium. Soft curves, generous whitespace.

**Hero copy pattern:**
- "{Business Name} — Beauty & Wellness in {City}"
- Sub: "Hair • Skin • Bridal • Professional care"

**Sections:**
1. Hero + "Book appointment"
2. Services grid (Haircut, Facial, Bridal, Nails — with short descriptions)
3. Gallery strip (3–4 placeholder images, salon interior style)
4. Why choose us
5. Testimonial-style quote (generic but local tone, no fake names required — use "Our clients love...")
6. Contact + hours

**Colors:** Blush, cream, soft gold, or sage green. Avoid gym-dark or tech-neon.

---

## Restaurant / café

**Feel:** Warm, appetizing, inviting.

**Hero:** "{Name} — {Cuisine type} in {City}"

**Sections:** Menu highlights, ambiance, chef story (short), hours, reserve CTA, location.

**Colors:** Warm earth tones, deep red, olive, wood textures.

---

## Clinic / dental / medical

**Feel:** Clean, trustworthy, professional.

**Hero:** "{Name} — Trusted healthcare in {City}"

**Sections:** Services, doctors/team, facilities, book consultation, hours, emergency line.

**Colors:** White, soft blue, teal. High readability. No flashy animations.

---

## Coaching / professional academy

**Feel:** Aspirational, credible.

**Hero:** "{Name} — {Subject} coaching in {City}"

**Sections:** Courses, outcomes, faculty, enrollment CTA, success metrics (generic ranges OK).

## Picking from `popular-web-designs`

| Business type | Borrow craft from | Do NOT copy literally |
|---------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Gym | Bold typography, strong CTAs | Stripe dashboard look |
| Salon | Editorial spacing, soft cards | Linear developer tool UI |
| Restaurant | Hero imagery layout | SaaS pricing tables |
| Clinic | Clean grids, trust badges | Crypto landing pages |

Use popular-web-designs for **spacing, font pairing, component polish** — then apply **category colors and copy** from this file.

## Copy from lead `issues_found`

| Issue in lead report | Show in demo |
|---------------------|-------------|
| No website | Full polished site — they're seeing the fix |
| No online booking | Prominent "Book now" / WhatsApp buttons |
| No WhatsApp | WhatsApp CTA in header + sticky mobile bar |
| Inactive social | Instagram-style gallery section (visual only) |
| Outdated website | Modern layout, mobile-first, fresh typography |

The demo is a **visual answer** to what you sell in `pitch-generator`.
